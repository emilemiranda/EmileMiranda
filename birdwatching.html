Bird in the Place Where you Live

Created for a UW-Madison Graphic Design in Cartography course, this infographic looks at the convergence of human populations with birdwatching observations submitted to eBird on Global Big Day 2018. This piece was created using ArcGIS Pro and Adobe Illustrator.
