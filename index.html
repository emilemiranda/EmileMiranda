Hi, I'm Nicki.

I make maps. I studied geography and GIS as an undergraduate, which sent me down the path to a Master's in Cartography and GIS from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Currently my geospatial work is in commercial poultry, where I blend my love of geography with a lifelong interest in agriculture. In my free time, I can usually be found birding and hanging out with my dog.

Bronchitis Early Warning System

Developed for the Georgia Poultry Laboratory Network and as my practicum project for the UW-Madison Master's in Cartography and GIS, this web-based report uses interactive maps and charts to provide key information on infectious bronchitis in Georgia's commercial poultry flocks. This project brought a static GPLN report that's been informing decision-makers for over a decade into an online platform able to reach a wider audience. Fully open-source, the interactives for this project are built on Leaflet and Chart.JS.
